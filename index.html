What we built

How the system responds

Automated nightly pipeline processes all new reviews before the business day starts
NLP preprocessing cleans and normalizes text for consistent, reliable analysis
Reviews automatically categorized by issue type — performance, usability, system failures, and more
Sentiment identified per review with explanations your analysts can trust and report on
Interactive dashboard with filters for rating, sentiment, category, and time range

How it works

From raw review to actionable insight

Every review passes through a five-stage automated pipeline, running nightly on Azure Machine Learning — delivering clean, structured intelligence before your team starts their day.

1

Ingest

New reviews collected with text, ratings, timestamps, and app identifiers

→
2

Clean

→
3

Analyze

Sentiment identified and issue category assigned per review

4

Predict

5

Surface

Results pushed to the dashboard, ready for filtering and reporting
